Installation instructions for Earnly Beta Testers

Welcome to the Earnly Beta Testing Program

Thank you for participating in our beta testing program. This guide will walk you through the process of installing and testing the Earnly app using Firebase App Distribution.

Note: Some screenshots in this guide show the Firebase interface in Ukrainian. The actual language you see may depend on your device settings.

Important: As mentioned in our testing plan, we'll be using Firebase App Distribution for secure beta testing. This requires a specific installation process that is different from typical app store downloads.

Please follow the steps below carefully to ensure successful installation of the test app.

Firebase App Distribution Process

1

Receive Email Invitation

You will receive an email invitation from Firebase App Distribution with the subject line "You're invited to test Earnly".

This email will contain a "Get Started" button that you need to click to begin the process.

2

Accept the Invitation

Important: Open the email invitation on your Android device.

Sign in with your Google account when prompted and click "Accept invitation".

You will be directed to a webpage where you'll need to sign in and accept the invitation to test the app.

3

Download Firebase App Tester

You will be prompted to download the Firebase App Tester app. This is a special app that manages beta test installations and is not available on Google Play Store.

Tap "DOWNLOAD" or "Завантажити App Tester" on the prompt that appears.

Note: If you don't see the download prompt, visit appdistribution.firebase.google.com directly on your Android device.

4

Enable Unknown Sources

You will see a security warning since the app is not from the Google Play Store.

You'll need to allow installation from unknown sources. Your device will show a message like "З міркувань безпеки на вашому телефоні зараз заборонено встановлювати невідомі додатки з цього джерела".

Tap "Settings" (Налаштування) to open your device's security settings, then enable the option to allow installations from this source.

5

Open Firebase App Tester

After installation completes, open the Firebase App Tester app.

Sign in with the same Google account you used to accept the invitation. You may need to grant permissions for the app to collect device information for testing purposes.

You will see a list of available test apps, including Earnly (co.uk.earnly.android).

6

Install Earnly Test App

In the Firebase App Tester, select the Earnly app and tap "DOWNLOAD" (Завантажити) next to the latest version (currently 1.0.13).

You may need to approve installation from unknown sources again, this time for the Firebase App Tester.

Tap "INSTALL" (Установити) to complete the installation.

7

Install from Unknown Sources

When you attempt to install the Earnly app, you may see another security prompt about installing apps from unknown sources.

Tap "SETTINGS" (Налаштування) and enable the permission for Firebase App Tester.

Return to the installation and tap "INSTALL" (Установити) to continue.

8

Launch and Test Earnly

The Earnly app will now be available on your device's home screen, showing the turquoise icon with an "E".

Open the app and log in with your test credentials (which will be provided separately).

Explore the app features according to the testing guidelines, with particular focus on the newly released News feature.

Important: The Firebase App Tester interface may show text in your device's system language. Common buttons and their translations:

  • Download = Завантажити
  • Install = Установити
  • Cancel = Скасувати
  • Settings = Налаштування
  • Continue = Продовжити

Common Issues and Troubleshooting

Cannot find Firebase App Tester on Google Play Store

The Firebase App Tester is not available on the Google Play Store. You must download it through the invitation email or by visiting appdistribution.firebase.google.com directly on your Android device.

Already accepted invitation but need to install on a different device

If you've already accepted the invitation but need to install the app on a different device, you can visit appdistribution.firebase.google.com directly on the new device. Sign in with the same Google account you used to accept the invitation.

Installation fails with "App not installed" error

This can happen if you previously installed a version of the app from a different source (like Google Play). To fix this issue:

  1. Uninstall any existing version of the Earnly app
  2. Ensure you have enough storage space on your device
  3. Try the installation process again
Cannot enable installation from unknown sources

The exact steps to enable installation from unknown sources vary by device manufacturer and Android version. Generally, you can:

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Go to Security or Privacy
  3. Look for "Install unknown apps" or "Unknown sources"
  4. Select your browser app and toggle the permission on

On some devices, you'll only be prompted to enable this when actually installing the app.

Not receiving notification emails about new builds

Check your spam folder to ensure notifications aren't being filtered. You can also check for new builds directly in the Firebase App Tester application by opening it and refreshing the app list.

Current Features to Test

The current version (1.0.13) includes the new News feature with the following functionality:

  • Personalized Financial Updates - Tax alerts and economic insights directly relevant to your financial situation
  • Multi-level Filtering System - Filter content by categories and subcategories
  • Navigation Tabs - Easily switch between All, My News, UK News, and World News sections
  • Year Filtering - Access historical content from 2022-2025 with a simple filter system
  • Dynamic Subcategories - Filter by specialized topics like Tax Updates, Budget News, Economy, and more
  • "How does this affect me?" feature - Shows personalized impact of tax and financial updates

When testing, please pay particular attention to the news filtering system and the personalization features. We'd like feedback on the relevance of the "How does this affect me?" calculations and the ease of finding information relevant to your specific financial situation.

Providing Feedback

Your feedback is crucial to improving the Earnly app before its public release. Please follow these guidelines when reporting issues or providing feedback:

  1. Use the in-app feedback option - Look for the feedback button in the app's settings menu
  2. Include detailed information - Device model, operating system version, app version, and steps to reproduce any issues
  3. Provide screenshots - Visual evidence helps us understand and fix problems more quickly
  4. Suggest improvements - We value your ideas about features and user experience
  5. Report issues promptly - The sooner we know about problems, the sooner we can fix them

You can also send feedback directly to adam.marsh@earnly.co.uk if you prefer email communication.
